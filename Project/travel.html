Driving Directions

From Jerez Airport (Journey time: approx 40 minutes)

1. From Jerez Airport follow the "Salida" signs and then pick up signs towards the A4/E5 motorway and head towards Cadiz.
2. At Junction 668 follow signs to Chiclana and Algeciras and join the A48/E5.
3. Exit at km 10 La Barossa. At the roundabout at the top of the slip road, take the second exit towards La Barrosa (You go past a large store on your right hand side called Polanco).
4. At the next roundabout take the first exit (right) onto Carretera Las Lagunas.
5. Continue to another roundabout and go straight on.
6. Turn onto the second road on the left (Camino de la Cerradura II). The villa is no 13 on the left hand side.

From Seville Airport (Journey time: approx 1 ¼ hours)

1. From Seville Airport head towards Cadiz (westbound) on the Ring Road.
2. At Junction 8 take the A4/E05 motorway southbound towards Cadiz & Jerez.
3. At Junction 668 follow Chiclana and Algeciras onto the NIV road.
4. At the end of the NIV road follow signs to Chiclana and Algeciras and join the A48/E5.
5. Exit at km 10 La Barossa. At the roundabout at the top of the slip road, take the second exit towards La Barossa (You go past a large store on your right hand side called Polanco).
6. At the next roundabout take the first exit, onto Carratera Las Lagunas.
7. Continue to another roundabout and go straight on.
8. Turn onto the second road on the left (Camino de la Cerradura II). The villa is no 13 on the left hand side.

From Malaga Airport (Journey time: approx. 2 ¼ hours)

1. Leave the airport and turn right onto Avenida de Velázquez, MA 21.
2. After about 2km join the E-15/A-7 Algeciras/Cádiz. Continue on this road for about 25 miles. (Just after Benalmadena, you will have the option to stay on this road, or to take the toll road E-15/AP-7 The toll road is much quicker, but costs about 15 euros (paid in 3 parts). If you take the toll road you will re-incorporate automatically to the E-15/A-7 Algeciras/Cádiz just after Estepona.
3. Exit at km 110B to join the A-381 towards Jerez/AP-4/Los Barrios/E- 5/Sevilla. You will be on this road for about ½ hour.
4. Exit at km 24 towards A-390/Paterna/A-389/A-2234/Chiclana/Medina. (Oeste)/Vejer/Arcos/A-396.
5. At the roundabout take the second exit A-390/Medina/Paterna/A-389/A-2234/Chiclana/Vejer/Arcos/A-396.
6. At the next roundabout take the second exit to join the A-390 Chiclana/Vejer/A-396.
7. Stay on the A-390 Chiclana for about 12 miles (You will go across another 2 roundabouts).
8. When you are approaching Chiclana, you will see a motorway flyover, you need to go under the bridge and take an immediate right to join the...
9. E-5/A-48 Málaga/Algeciras. (The slip road is immediately after the flyover, almost under the bridge)
10. Leave the E-5/A-48 at km 10 towards Chiclana de la Frontera/N-340a.
11. At the roundabout take the second exit onto N-340a. (You will pass the front of a big furniture store called Polanco)
12. At the next roundabout turn right onto Ctra de las Lagunas.
13. Continue on this road for about 2 miles. At the roundabout, go straight on.
14. The villa is on the second street on the left after the roundabout. (Camino de la Cerradura II, 13)
