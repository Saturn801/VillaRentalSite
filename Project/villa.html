Villa

Overview:

This large modern villa is set in extensive secure grounds, accessed through an electric gate. It is only a few minutes´ walk from glorious sandy beaches, watersports, restaurants and bars. It is very well equipped and furnished to a high standard.

You will find two large covered furnished patio areas, suitable for dining and relaxing in the shade, and a large open patio for sunbathing. There is a large lawn, a fantastic pool and a hot-tub. Please note that the pool is not fenced off, so young children need to be supervised at all times. Next to the dining patio you will find a large brick built barbeque.

The accommodation is all on one level, allowing easy access for those with mobility issues.

The open plan living/dining area is over 55m2. There are two large leather sofas with additional lounge seating for up to 10 people and very large dining table which seats 10. There is a 42" television with satellite television, a dvd player and an ipod docking station for your enjoyment.

The kitchen is very well equipped with all modern conveniences; dishwasher, microwave, coffee maker etc. and an American fridge freezer. A separate utility room has a washing machine, tumble dryer and an additional freezer.

There are four bedrooms in total, all en-suite; Two with king-sized beds and room for an additional single bed and a cot, one twin and one triple.

There is a second lounge/games-room which hosts a video games area and plenty of board games, dvd´s and books.

The villa is fully air-conditioned . Wireless broadband is available at no additional cost and hire of other office equipment can be arranged.

The villa is located on a tarmac road, so there is no problem with rental car insurance premiums, which you will experience with many other properties in the area, located on rough tracks.

All bed linen and towels are included in the price. Beach towels are also provided.
Travel cots and highchairs can be provided at no cost, but must be pre-booked.
A selection of toys, games, dvds and books are provided for your enjoyment. There is a dart board, basketball net and badminton net and plenty of bicycles and children's garden toys are provided.
